My City Bhagalpur

INTRODUCTION

Bhagalpur, known as the "Silk City" of India, is located in the state of Bihar. It is famous for its rich cultural heritage, ancient history, and especially for producing high-quality Tussar silk. The city lies on the southern banks of the holy river Ganga and is one of the oldest districts in Bihar.

CULTURE

The city has a mix of modern and traditional life. People here are warm and welcoming. Bhagalpur is home to Vikramshila University ruins, an ancient center of learning that attracts many visitors interested in history and archaeology. The city also boasts beautiful gardens, temples, and vibrant markets.

FESTIVALS

Bhagalpur's festivals are celebrated with great enthusiasm, especially Chhath Puja, which brings families together to pray to the Sun God on the riverbanks. With its educational institutions, growing infrastructure, and unique charm, Bhagalpur continues to evolve while preserving its rich traditions.
